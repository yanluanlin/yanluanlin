Haoyu Tong
ABOUT
I am interested in understanding how precipitation and its associated mesoscale convective systems (MCSs) and tropical cyclones (TCs) respond to global warming.
Beyond this, my work also explores AI-based weather forecasting, with an emphasis on data-driven methods for improving the prediction of high-impact weather events.
